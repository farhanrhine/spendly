Privacy & Security

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your financial information.

Information We Collect

We collect financial transaction data you input into the application to help you track your expenses. This includes transaction amounts, dates, categories, descriptions, and other information you voluntarily provide. We do not collect information about your device or browsing habits outside of the Finlo application.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide you with expense tracking, financial insights, and analytics within the Finlo application. We use this information to help you understand your spending patterns and make better financial decisions. We never use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose outside of providing our core service.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard encryption to protect your financial data from unauthorized access. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using secure protocols. We implement strict access controls and regular security audits to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or other external organizations. We may only share data when legally required by law enforcement or court order.

Data Retention

Your financial data is retained as long as your account remains active. If you choose to delete your account, all associated transaction data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. We do not maintain backups of deleted personal data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You can export your transaction history directly from the application. If you wish to exercise any of these rights or have questions about your data, please contact us through the application.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of Finlo following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or how we handle your information, please reach out to us through the Finlo application or website. We are committed to transparency and addressing any privacy concerns you may have.
